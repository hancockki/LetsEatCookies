Pumpkin Puree Heath Bars Cookies
1 cup all-purpose flour
2 egg
0.5 cup granulated sugar
0.25 cup brown sugar
0.5 cup butter
0.75 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
0.5 teaspoon baking powder
2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
6 tablespoon pumpkin puree
1 teaspoon cornstarch
0.75 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
4 ounce cream cheese
4 ounce chocolate chips
1 cup white chocolate chips
0.75 cup dried cranberries
1 chopped Oreos
6 tablespoon sweet potato
0.5 cup pistachios
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
0.5 cup heath bars
0.5 cup pistachio
1 vanilla

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Whisk the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and both sugars together until smooth. Add 2 eggs and the rest of the wet ingredients.
4. Add your wet ingredients to the dry ingredients until combined.
5. Add the pure vanilla extract, pumpkin puree, cornstarch, pumpkin pie spice, cream cheese, chocolate chips, white chocolate chips, dried cranberries, chopped Oreos, sweet potato, pistachios, ground cinnamon, heath bars, pistachio, and vanilla and mix until all combined, and a dough forms.
6. Roll the cookie dough into medium-sized scoops and place 3 inches apart on the baking sheet.
7. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until edges appear set.
8. Remove from the oven and allow to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool.
9. Enjoy!
