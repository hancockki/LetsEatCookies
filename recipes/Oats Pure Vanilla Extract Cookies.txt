Oats Pure Vanilla Extract Cookies
2 cup all-purpose flour
2 egg
1 cup granulated sugar
0.75 cup brown sugar
0.75 cup butter
0.75 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
0.75 dried cranberries
 your choice of icing
3 cup oats
0.75 cup banana
1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
1 cup chocolate chips
1 tablespoon molasses
2 teaspoon cornstarch
0.75 cup white chocolate morsels
0.5 teaspoon ground cinnamon
0.5 cup hazelnut
 sanding sugar sprinkles
0.5 teaspoon almond extract
0.5 cup pistachios
1 cup M&Ms
0.5 cup brazil nut

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Whisk the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and both sugars together until smooth. Add 2 eggs and the rest of the wet ingredients.
4. Add your wet ingredients to the dry ingredients until combined.
5. Add the dried cranberries, your choice of icing, oats, banana, pure vanilla extract, chocolate chips, molasses, cornstarch, white chocolate morsels, ground cinnamon, hazelnut, sanding sugar sprinkles, almond extract, pistachios, M&Ms, and brazil nut and mix until all combined, and a dough forms.
6. Roll the cookie dough into medium-sized scoops and place 3 inches apart on the baking sheet.
7. Bake for 8-15 minutes or until edges appear set.
8. Remove from the oven and allow to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool.
9. Enjoy!
