White Chocolate Morsels Your Choice Of Icing Cookies
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 egg
0.5 cup granulated sugar
0.5 cup brown sugar
1 cup butter
0.75 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
0.5 teaspoon baking powder
3 cup dates
0.75 dried cranberries
1 cup white chocolate chips
3 cup oats
0.75 cup white chocolate morsels
0.5 teaspoon almond extract
1 teaspoon cinnamon
2 teaspoon vanilla
 sanding sugar sprinkles
2 teaspoon cornstarch
1 tablespoon molasses
 your choice of icing
1 chopped Oreos
3 cup brazil nut

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Whisk the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and both sugars together until smooth. Add 1 egg and the rest of the wet ingredients.
4. Add your wet ingredients to the dry ingredients until combined.
5. Add the dates, dried cranberries, white chocolate chips, oats, white chocolate morsels, almond extract, cinnamon, vanilla, sanding sugar sprinkles, cornstarch, molasses, your choice of icing, chopped Oreos, and brazil nut and mix until all combined, and a dough forms.
6. Roll the cookie dough into medium-sized scoops and place 3 inches apart on the baking sheet.
7. Bake for 10-10 minutes or until edges appear set.
8. Remove from the oven and allow to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool.
9. Enjoy!
