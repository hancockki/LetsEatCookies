Yummy Cookies
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 egg
0.75 cup granulated sugar
0.75 cup brown sugar
1 cup butter
0.5 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
0.25 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Whisk the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and both sugars together until smooth. Add 1 egg and the rest of the wet ingredients.
4. Add your wet ingredients to the dry ingredients until combined.
5. Make sure you mix until a dough forms.
6. Roll the cookie dough into medium-sized scoops and place 3 inches apart on the baking sheet.
7. Bake for 8-11 minutes or until edges appear set.
8. Remove from the oven and allow to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool.
9. Enjoy!
