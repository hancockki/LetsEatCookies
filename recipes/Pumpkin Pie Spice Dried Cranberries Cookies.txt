Pumpkin Pie Spice Dried Cranberries Cookies
2 cup all-purpose flour
1 egg
0.25 cup granulated sugar
0.5 cup brown sugar
0.75 cup butter
 salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
6 tablespoon pumpkin puree
1 teaspoon ground cardamom
0.5 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon cream of tartar
0.5 cup hazelnut
1 cup white chocolate chips
1 teaspoon cornstarch
1 cup chocolate chips
0.5 cup heath bars
0.75 cup dried cranberries
6 tablespoon sweet potato
1 teaspoon ground ginger
0.75 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
0.5 teaspoon ground allspice
0.5 cup pistachios
6 tablespoon pumpkin

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Whisk the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and both sugars together until smooth. Add 1 egg and the rest of the wet ingredients.
4. Add your wet ingredients to the dry ingredients until combined.
5. Add the pure vanilla extract, pumpkin puree, ground cardamom, ground cinnamon, cream of tartar, hazelnut, white chocolate chips, cornstarch, chocolate chips, heath bars, dried cranberries, sweet potato, ground ginger, pumpkin pie spice, ground allspice, pistachios, and pumpkin and mix until all combined, and a dough forms.
6. Roll the cookie dough into medium-sized scoops and place 3 inches apart on the baking sheet.
7. Bake for 7-14 minutes or until edges appear set.
8. Remove from the oven and allow to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool.
9. Enjoy!
