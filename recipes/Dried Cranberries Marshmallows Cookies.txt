Dried Cranberries Marshmallows Cookies
1 cup all-purpose flour
2 egg
0.5 cup granulated sugar
1 cup brown sugar
0.5 cup butter
0.5 teaspoon salt
0.5 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
2 teaspoon vanilla
2 tablespoon milk
1 cup raisins
0.75 cup white chocolate morsels
1 chopped Oreos
2 teaspoon chocolate
1 teaspoon cinnamon
12 marshmallows
2 tablespoon honey
0.5 cup pistachios
0.75 dried cranberries
2 teaspoon banana
1 cup white chocolate chips
1 teaspoon cornstarch
4 ounce cream cheese
0.333333333333 cup graham cracker crumbs
0.5 cup chocolate chips
2 cup oats
1 tablespoon molasses

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Whisk the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and both sugars together until smooth. Add 2 eggs and the rest of the wet ingredients.
4. Add your wet ingredients to the dry ingredients until combined.
5. Add the vanilla, milk, raisins, white chocolate morsels, chopped Oreos, chocolate, cinnamon, marshmallows, honey, pistachios, dried cranberries, banana, white chocolate chips, cornstarch, cream cheese, graham cracker crumbs, chocolate chips, oats, and molasses and mix until all combined, and a dough forms.
6. Roll the cookie dough into medium-sized scoops and place 3 inches apart on the baking sheet.
7. Bake for 8-11 minutes or until edges appear set.
8. Remove from the oven and allow to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool.
9. Enjoy!
