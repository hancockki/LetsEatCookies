Biscoff Spread Graham Cracker Crumbs Cookies
1 cup all-purpose flour
2 egg
1 cup granulated sugar
0.5 cup brown sugar
0.5 cup butter
0.75 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
0.5 cup Biscoff spread
2 cup dates
0.5 teaspoon almond extract
1 teaspoon cornstarch
0.5 cup vanilla
0.333333333333 cup graham cracker crumbs
3 cup oats
1 chocolate
12 marshmallows
0.5 cup chocolate chips
1 tablespoon molasses
 sanding sugar sprinkles
1 cup raisins

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Whisk the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and both sugars together until smooth. Add 2 eggs and the rest of the wet ingredients.
4. Add your wet ingredients to the dry ingredients until combined.
5. Add the Biscoff spread, dates, almond extract, cornstarch, vanilla, graham cracker crumbs, oats, chocolate, marshmallows, chocolate chips, molasses, sanding sugar sprinkles, and raisins and mix until all combined, and a dough forms.
6. Roll the cookie dough into medium-sized scoops and place 3 inches apart on the baking sheet.
7. Bake for 7-12 minutes or until edges appear set.
8. Remove from the oven and allow to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool.
9. Enjoy!
