Sanding Sugar Sprinkles Cream Cheese Cookies
3 cup all-purpose flour
1 egg
0.25 cup granulated sugar
0.25 cup brown sugar
10 tablespoon butter
0.25 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
0.75 cup dates
0.5 teaspoon almond extract
0.5 cup chocolate chips
0.5 cup almond
6 ounce bittersweet chocolate
 sanding sugar sprinkles
0.75 cup dried cranberries
1 teaspoon cinnamon
2 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup white chocolate chips
1 teaspoon cornstarch
4 ounce cream cheese
 your choice of icing
0.75 cup carob

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Whisk the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and both sugars together until smooth. Add 1 egg and the rest of the wet ingredients.
4. Add your wet ingredients to the dry ingredients until combined.
5. Add the dates, almond extract, chocolate chips, almond, bittersweet chocolate, sanding sugar sprinkles, dried cranberries, cinnamon, vanilla, white chocolate chips, cornstarch, cream cheese, your choice of icing, and carob and mix until all combined, and a dough forms.
6. Roll the cookie dough into medium-sized scoops and place 3 inches apart on the baking sheet.
7. Bake for 9-12 minutes or until edges appear set.
8. Remove from the oven and allow to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool.
9. Enjoy!
