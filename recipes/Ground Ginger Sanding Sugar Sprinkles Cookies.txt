Ground Ginger Sanding Sugar Sprinkles Cookies
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 egg
0.25 cup granulated sugar
0.75 cup brown sugar
0.75 cup butter
10 tablespoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
0.5 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon ground cardamom
 your choice of icing
1 teaspoon cream of tartar
0.5 cup Biscoff spread
 sanding sugar sprinkles
2 teaspoon chocolate
1 teaspoon ground ginger
0.5 cup vanilla
0.5 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon cornstarch
0.5 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Whisk the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and both sugars together until smooth. Add 1 egg and the rest of the wet ingredients.
4. Add your wet ingredients to the dry ingredients until combined.
5. Add the ground cardamom, your choice of icing, cream of tartar, Biscoff spread, sanding sugar sprinkles, chocolate, ground ginger, vanilla, cinnamon, cornstarch, and chocolate chips and mix until all combined, and a dough forms.
6. Roll the cookie dough into medium-sized scoops and place 3 inches apart on the baking sheet.
7. Bake for 7-12 minutes or until edges appear set.
8. Remove from the oven and allow to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool.
9. Enjoy!
