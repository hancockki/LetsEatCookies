Sanding Sugar Sprinkles Banana Cookies
2 cup all-purpose flour
1 egg
0.25 cup granulated sugar
0.5 cup brown sugar
0.5 cup butter
0.25 teaspoon salt
0.5 teaspoon baking soda
0.25 teaspoon baking powder
2 teaspoon banana
 your choice of icing
0.333333333333 cup graham cracker crumbs
 sanding sugar sprinkles
1 cup chocolate chips
2 teaspoon vanilla
2 teaspoon cornstarch
12 marshmallows
0.5 teaspoon almond extract
2 teaspoon chocolate
0.333333333333 cup peppermint

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Whisk the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and both sugars together until smooth. Add 1 egg and the rest of the wet ingredients.
4. Add your wet ingredients to the dry ingredients until combined.
5. Add the banana, your choice of icing, graham cracker crumbs, sanding sugar sprinkles, chocolate chips, vanilla, cornstarch, marshmallows, almond extract, chocolate, and peppermint and mix until all combined, and a dough forms.
6. Roll the cookie dough into medium-sized scoops and place 3 inches apart on the baking sheet.
7. Bake for 7-14 minutes or until edges appear set.
8. Remove from the oven and allow to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool.
9. Enjoy!
