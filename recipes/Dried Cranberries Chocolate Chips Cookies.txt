Dried Cranberries Chocolate Chips Cookies
1 cup all-purpose flour
2 egg
0.5 cup granulated sugar
1 cup brown sugar
 butter
0.75 teaspoon salt
0.5 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
6 ounce bittersweet chocolate
0.5 cup heath bars
6 tablespoon pumpkin puree
0.75 cup dried cranberries
1 teaspoon cornstarch
1 cup chocolate chips
0.5 cup fig
0.5 cup potato chips
0.75 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Whisk the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and both sugars together until smooth. Add 2 eggs and the rest of the wet ingredients.
4. Add your wet ingredients to the dry ingredients until combined.
5. Add the bittersweet chocolate, heath bars, pumpkin puree, dried cranberries, cornstarch, chocolate chips, fig, potato chips, and pumpkin pie spice and mix until all combined, and a dough forms.
6. Roll the cookie dough into medium-sized scoops and place 3 inches apart on the baking sheet.
7. Bake for 10-13 minutes or until edges appear set.
8. Remove from the oven and allow to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool.
9. Enjoy!
