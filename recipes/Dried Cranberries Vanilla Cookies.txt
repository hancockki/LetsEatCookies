Dried Cranberries Vanilla Cookies
2 cup all-purpose flour
1 egg
0.25 cup granulated sugar
0.75 cup brown sugar
 butter
10 tablespoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon baking powder
 sanding sugar sprinkles
0.333333333333 cup graham cracker crumbs
3 cup oats
0.75 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
1 teaspoon cornstarch
1 teaspoon cinnamon
0.75 dried cranberries
1 tablespoon molasses
6 tablespoon pumpkin puree
0.5 cup almond
6 ounce bittersweet chocolate
0.333333333333 cup licorice
12 marshmallows
6 ounce vanilla

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Whisk the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and both sugars together until smooth. Add 1 egg and the rest of the wet ingredients.
4. Add your wet ingredients to the dry ingredients until combined.
5. Add the sanding sugar sprinkles, graham cracker crumbs, oats, pumpkin pie spice, cornstarch, cinnamon, dried cranberries, molasses, pumpkin puree, almond, bittersweet chocolate, licorice, marshmallows, and vanilla and mix until all combined, and a dough forms.
6. Roll the cookie dough into medium-sized scoops and place 3 inches apart on the baking sheet.
7. Bake for 7-11 minutes or until edges appear set.
8. Remove from the oven and allow to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool.
9. Enjoy!
